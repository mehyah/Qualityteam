Purpose

The purpose of this Standard Operating Procedure is to establish a clear and consistent framework for how quality assurance activities are conducted within the organization...

Scope

This SOP applies to all quality assurance activities conducted by the Quality team across all departments within the organization, including but not limited to Sales, Negotiations, Customer Service, and Payment Processing...

Definitions and Abbreviations

Term/AbbreviationMeaning
Call auditAn audit done by Quality Analyst to an inbound/outbound call.
Non-call auditAudit for departments that don't interact with clients directly.
ScorecardA tool used to evaluate and measure performance based on criteria.
Redflag/AutofailCritical error that results in an automatic failure.
CRFCoaching Form Request used for requesting a coaching session.
ADFAudit Dispute Form submitted for contesting audit results.
SMGSuccess Matrix Grid, a monthly performance scorecard.

Roles and Responsibilities

Quality Analyst

  • Conducts call and non-call audits
  • Submits reports and attends weekly meetings
  • Provides coaching, training, and participates in calibration

Senior Quality Analyst

  • Leads the team, provides guidance, performs audit reviews
  • Handles special audits, team performance tracking, and disputes

Quality Manager

  • Validates broken processes, leads performance reviews
  • Defines KPIs, drives improvements, coordinates with operations
